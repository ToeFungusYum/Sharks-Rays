Sharks & Rays


Sharks & Rays have been on the earth for thousands of years and are both very unique.

  • Differences

    		•
    • Sharks

      • Can have 5-7 rows of gill openings

      • Most have a mouth located toward the front of their head

      • Most use a side-to-side motion which they use to swim through the water
    • Rays

      • Can only have five rows of gill openings

      • Most have a mouth located on their underside

      • Most swim in wave-like motions to swim through the water
